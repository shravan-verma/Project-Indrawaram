Our Story

A Tamil kitchen that
never sleeps.

The Indrawaram Cafe began with a simple belief — that Greater Noida deserved South Indian food the way it's made back home. Batter fermented overnight. Chutneys ground fresh. Coffee pulled tall between davara and tumbler until it froths just right.

Today, we're one of the largest South Indian cafes in the city — and one of the only ones open all 24 hours. Whether it's a 6 a.m. idli before the metro or a 2 a.m. ghee roast after a long shift, our kitchen is lit and the tawa is hot.
